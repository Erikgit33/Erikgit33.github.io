Sweden

Sweden is a country in Northern Europe. It borders Norway and Finland, and is connected to Denmark by a bridge-tunnel across the 'Ã–resund'. At 450,295 square kilometers, Sweden is the third largest country in the European Union by area, with a total population of about 10,6 million. Sweden has a low population density of 23,7 inhabitants per square kilometer, but with a considerably higher density in the southern half of the country. Approximately 85% of the population lives in urban areas.
